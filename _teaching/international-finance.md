This MBA lecture introduces participants to core concepts in international financial markets, exchange rate dynamics, and cross-border investment decisions. It provides an analytical understanding of global financial integration and its implications for corporate decision-making.

## Course Content

1. Exchange Rate Determination and Forecasting  
2. International Parity Conditions  
3. Currency Risk and Hedging Strategies  
4. International Capital Markets  
5. Cross-Border Investment and Financing Decisions

## Learning Outcomes

- Understand the functioning of global financial markets.  
- Analyze exchange rate movements and manage currency risk.  
- Evaluate international investment opportunities and funding strategies.

## Target Audience

MBA students seeking a global perspective on financial decision-making.
