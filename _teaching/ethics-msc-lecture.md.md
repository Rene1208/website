This lecture explores ethical frameworks, sustainability principles, and their implications for financial markets and
corporate decision-making. Students develop an understanding of responsible management and the societal impacts of
financial activity.

## Course Content

1. Ethical Theories and Decision-Making
2. Sustainability in Finance
3. ESG Metrics and Reporting Standards
4. Ethical Investment Strategies

## Learning Outcomes

- Apply ethical reasoning to financial decisions.
- Understand sustainability metrics and their relevance to modern finance.
- Critically evaluate ESG strategies and their limitations.

## Target Audience

Graduate students in banking and finance with an interest in sustainability-oriented financial practices.
