Supervision of master's theses in the areas of banking, finance, behavioral economics, and sustainability. Guidance is
provided along every stage of the research project.

## Responsibilities

1. Topic Identification and Formulation of Research Questions
2. Methodological Coaching (Empirical or Experimental)
3. Feedback on Drafts and Analysis
4. Final Evaluation and Grading

## Focus Areas

- Financial Markets
- Behavioral and Experimental Finance
- ESG
- Meta-Science and Open Science in Economics and Finance

## Impact

Supporting graduate students in producing rigorous, original research that contributes to their academic and
professional development.
