<div style="text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-word;">I presented work on "Do MTurkers Exhibit Myopic Loss Aversion?", a highly powered online experiment with 937 participants on Amazon Mechanical Turk. The study examines whether participants exhibit behavior consistent with myopic loss aversion across two risk profiles and discusses where evidence for MLA appears in screened sub-samples.</div>
